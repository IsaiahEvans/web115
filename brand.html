Brand Guide

Mission

Streamline Studios delivers cinematic car and lifestyle visuals with a clean, minimal UI and bold accent moments.

Logo & Favicon

  • Placement: Top-left in the header; nav stays centered.
  • Clear space: At least the height of the “S” around the mark.
  • Background: Transparent; avoid placing on busy photos.
  • Favicon: Provide favicon.ico, 32×32, 16×16, 180×180 (Apple), and 512×512.

Colors

  • #0f1115 — Ink / Headings / Text
  • #ff6a00 — Accent / Buttons / Highlights
  • #ffffff — Background
  • #e5e7eb — Borders

Typography

  • Headings: Poppins 800/600
  • Body: Inter 400/600
  • Scale: h1 uses clamp for responsive sizes; line-height ~1.25

Voice & Theme

Energetic, confident, minimal. Two on-brand words for exercises:

  • Word 1: Turbo
  • Word 2: Traction

UI Elements

  • Links: Accent color with subtle underline on hover.
  • Buttons: Border in ink; hover adds a light background.
  • Cards/Blocks: Rounded corners (10px), light border #e5e7eb.

Imagery

High-contrast automotive photography, matte finishes, shallow depth of field, and warm accent lighting.

Do / Don’t

  • Do keep layouts clean with generous spacing.
  • Do use the accent sparingly for CTAs and highlights.
  • Don’t stretch or recolor the logo.
  • Don’t place text on top of the accent stripe unless contrast is AAA.
